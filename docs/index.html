Why dscssOnline?

In the age of fake news and social distancing, it's more important than ever to really get in touch with other human beeings and their opinions and experiences in a respectful way. Listening to each other and exploring and accepting different points of views helps us to get a better understanding of each other and overcome trolling and hate speech.

With dscssOnline (pronounce discuss online) you can search topics of your interest and instantly join an appropriate, moderated discussion by video or audio meeting. If you can't find your topic you can simply create a new discussion and invite others to join you.

Following things are important for a great and enriching discussion:

Get in direct touch

Talking to each other, eye to eye, helps to prevent misunderstandings.

Respect each other

Without respect my discussion partners will not be willing to hear my point of view.

Use concise topics

No one wants to waste time with unfocused and broad discussions.

Get to the point

This way the others can easily follow you.

Argue

Arguments based on each other can strengthen your point. Flaming does not.

Calm down and listen

A dialog only arises when you listen to each other. And it's the best way to find an acurate counterargument.
